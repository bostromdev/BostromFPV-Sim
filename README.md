# BostromFPV

> ⚠️ Early Alpha Build
>
> currently in active development. Features, physics, controls, performance, and flight characteristics may change between releases.

BostromFPV is a realistic FPV drone simulator focused on freestyle flying, responsive controls, and realistic quadcopter behavior. The goal is to provide a simple simulator that feels close to a real FPV drone while remaining lightweight and accessible on lower-end hardware.
Graphics will be updated and maps increased eventually.
## Features

- Realistic FPV flight physics
- Controller support
- Automatic control mapping
- Adjustable deadband settings
- Freestyle-focused flight experience
- Lightweight performance
- Windows, Linux, and macOS support

## Getting Started

### Controller Setup

1. Connect your controller before launching the simulator.
2. Open the **Controls** menu.
3. Select **Map Controls**.
4. Follow the prompts and move the requested stick, switch, or axis.
5. Add deadband if your controller inputs jitter while centered.
6. Click **Save Mapping** when finished.

### Important Arm Switch Note

When calibrating or assigning your arm switch:

1. Move the switch into the **ARMED** position.
2. Click **Save Mapping** while the switch remains armed.
3. Immediately move the switch back to the **DISARMED** position.
4. Exit the controls screen.

Failure to return the switch to the disarmed position may cause the drone to start armed the next time you enter a flight session.

## Basic Controls

| Control | Function |
|----------|----------|
| Throttle | Increase or decrease altitude |
| Roll | Tilt left or right |
| Pitch | Move forward or backward |
| Yaw | Rotate left or right |
| Arm Switch | Arm or disarm the drone |

## Flying Tips

- Start with small stick movements.
- Practice hovering before attempting freestyle tricks.
- Add deadband if the drone drifts while your sticks are centered.
- Lower rates for beginners and increase them as you improve.
- Practice lines, dives, power loops, and proximity flying.

## Troubleshooting

### Drone moves by itself

Increase stick deadband slightly and recalibrate controls.

### Controller not detected

Connect the controller before launching the simulator and remap controls.

### Axis appears reversed

Open the controls menu and remap the affected axis.
